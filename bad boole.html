About Us

The Light Rail Manila Corporation (LRMC) is the private operator of the LRT-1 system in Metro Manila, Philippines. Established in 2014, LRMC is a joint venture between Metro Pacific Investments Corporation, Ayala Corporation, Sumitomo Corporation, and Macquarie Investments Holdings. Under a 32-year concession agreement with the Department of Transportation and Light Rail Transit Authority, LRMC is responsible for the operation, maintenance, and development of the LRT-1 line, including the ongoing Cavite Extension project. LRMC partners with global companies like RATP, ALSTOM, Bouygues, and SYSTRA to enhance its services and infrastructure. Its mission is to become the commuters' choice for public transportation in the region.
